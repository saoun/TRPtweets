The People, Places, and Things Donald Trump Has Insulted on Twitter

We came across this piece by the New York Times, and decided it would make for a pretty neat visualization project. We wanted to get a better sense of the gender breakdown and different types of folks that Trump had insulted on Twitter.

We wrote a javascript scraper to pull recent data from the NYT, and visualized it all with D3.
